Research Philosophy

I got into this trying to understand how Web3 could work for communities that traditional finance ignores. Turns out the answer involves understanding how systems break.

The stablecoin research: you can't have perfect decentralization AND stability AND capital efficiency. Pick two. Attack modeling: vulnerabilities live between systems—oracle-circuit breaker interactions that look safe individually but break when combined.

I can model economic attacks and explain them to central bank officials. I design regenerative finance products and stress-test them with adversarial scenarios. Currently building the "DeFi Infrastructures for Regeneration Market" series.
